## Interview Questions

### Closed-ended Questions

**Q1 – Functional Preference**
Which feature do you think would be more helpful for saving your planning time: the advanced multi-filter system or the automated DIY itinerary builder?

Follow-up: Why do you think that specific feature is more valuable to you? In what situation would you use it the most?

**Q2 – Usage Pattern**
During a short trip (e.g., 3 days), are you more likely to follow the recommended route strictly, or do you prefer to frequently adjust the plan while you are on the go?

Follow-up: What kind of unexpected situation (e.g., weather, fatigue, or finding a new place) usually makes you change your original plans?

**Q3 – Workflow Preference**
When using our platform, would you prefer to build a food itinerary from scratch using the DIY tool, or would you rather pick a shared route from another user and then modify it to fit your needs?

Follow-up: What would make you trust a shared route enough to use it as your starting template?

**Q4 – Value Validation**
Does the combination of DIY route building and referencing shared itineraries make the planning process easier and more useful for users compared to traditional methods?

Follow-up: What is the biggest thing traditional methods (like Google Maps or blogs) are missing that this platform successfully provides?

### Open-ended Questions

**Q5 – Trust & Social Factor**
What would motivate you to share your own DIY route with other users, and what kind of details in a shared itinerary would make you trust another user’s recommendation?

Follow-up: If a stranger shared a route, what specific "proof" (like photos or detailed descriptions) would make it feel more authentic to you?

**Q6 – Interaction Logic**
If you are not satisfied with a specific restaurant in the generated route, how would you prefer to modify it (e.g., manually dragging and dropping another spot on the map, or having the system re-suggest a different one)?

Follow-up: Why do you prefer that specific way of interacting with the map over the other?

**Q7 – Decision Support**
When looking at the "Route 1, 2, 3" options, what key information (e.g., total walking distance, estimated waiting time at restaurants, or total cost) do you need most to help you quickly choose the best route?

Follow-up: If two routes were almost identical, which one of those factors would be the "dealbreaker" for you?

**Q8 – Requirement Gathering**
Besides the current options for location, safety index and route selection, what other specific factors (such as budget, group size, or dietary restrictions) would you expect the system to consider when generating your "Food Journey"?

Follow-up: How would your planning needs change if you were traveling solo versus traveling with a group of friends?
